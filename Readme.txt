HabitTracker — iPhone & Apple WatchKey Features- Add habit with title and scheduled time on iPhone and also on watch.- Delete habits on iPhone and reorder by scheduled time.- Mark habits as completed on iPhone and Apple Watch.- Persistent local storage using `UserDefaults` so habits persist across launches.- View and add habits directly from the Apple Watch.- Two-way synchronization between iPhone and Watch using `WatchConnectivity`.- Time-based notifications (watch sends reminders at scheduled times).- Notifications are cancelled automatically if a habit is completed.- Background refresh on the Watch to keep data and complications up to date.- Watch complication showing completed vs. total habits for quick progress checks.- Automatic daily reset: at the start of a new day all habits reset to incomplete.Architecture & Technologies- UI: SwiftUI on both iPhone and Apple Watch targets.- Communication: WatchConnectivity for two-way syncing of habit data.- Persistence: `UserDefaults` for lightweight local storage.- Notifications: Local notifications scheduled by the Watch to remind users.- Background Tasks & Complications: Background refresh support and a WidgetKit complication to display habit progress.ScreenshotsAll required screenshots for the assignment are included in the `Screenshots` folder.
